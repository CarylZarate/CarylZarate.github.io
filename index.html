Fabrication

We fabricate tools, machines, equipment and process to make argiculture, mining and for all other endeavors in the Cordilleras and beyond easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Design

We design tools, machines, equipment and process to make argiculture, mining and for all other endeavors in the Cordilleras and beyond easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Installation

We install tools, machines, equipment and process to make argiculture, mining and for all other endeavors in the Cordilleras and beyond easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Pattents

We write pattents for tools, machines, equipment and process to make argiculture, mining and for all other endeavors in the Philippines easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Maintenance

We maintain tools, machines, equipment and process to make argiculture, mining and for all other endeavors in the Cordilleras and beyond easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Mission 1

Primarily to invent/innovate tools, machines, equipment and process to make argiculture in the Cordilleras easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Mission 2

Secondly to invent/innovate tools, machines, equipment, processes to make small scale mining in the Cordilleras easier, less costly, more productive, environment friendly and sustainable.

Invent Innovate

Our vision is to be the invention and innovation hub of the Cordilleras that would make Cordilleran industries more efficient, productive, eco-friendly and sustainable.

Mission 3

Third to invent/innovate tools, machines, equipment, processes or formualas for all other endeavors in the Cordilleras making use of technologies that are available to us at the present and in the future.

Humble Begginings

On September 11, 2001, an inventors/innovators group from the Cordilleras was sponsored by DOST-CAR to a National Inventor’s competition at DOST, Taguig, Metro Manila. From that experience the group tried to seek the assistance of DOST-CAR for support to be able to develop their inventions and innovations.

Team and HQ

Sometime in August of 2012, Dr. Julius Caesar V. Sicat, Regional Director of DOST-CAR informed the group, then consisting of old and new members, to incorporate themselves to be able to avail of assistance in terms of an equipped Common Service Facility (CSF) where prototyping of inventions and innovations could be accomplished.

Legitimization

The group then incorporated as FISCAR Inc. on September 24, 2013. Finally on December 28, 2013 a MOA regarding said CSF was signed between DOST-CAR and FISCAR Inc.

Infinity and Beyond

As a guide for inventions and innovations for agriculture, an aquaponics system has been chosen to be FISCAR’s central theme. Where research and development (inventions /innovations) would be geared towards making the aquaponic system highly productive and sustainable. Scope of research would be from inputs to process and finally to outputs.
